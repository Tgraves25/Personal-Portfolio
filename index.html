E-commerce Website

The Cookware Hub is a sleek and responsive e-commerce website designed to showcase and sell premium kitchenware. It features a modern and intuitive layout with a navigation bar that allows easy browsing of products and access to contact information. The product showcase section highlights various cookware items with detailed images, prices, and descriptions.

ClimaSense Weather App

ClimaSense is a sleek, user-friendly weather forecasting app that lets users check the current weather by entering their city and state. Built with responsive design, it works seamlessly on all devices. Using data from the National Weather Service, ClimaSense delivers accurate and real-time weather updates, making it the perfect tool for staying informed on the go.
